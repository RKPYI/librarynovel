I Am The Fated Villain

Chapter 332, Even if That Is the Future, I’m Fine With It; You Dare To Injure Her?

=+=

Translator: Fate

Proofreader: Silavin

 

“Why are you here?” Sky King Zhi Yang furrowed his brow, “This isn’t a place for you should be in. You should go back.”

 

However, Gu Xianer had already seen through what these two were scheming and remained unmoved. Her eyes were frigid, emitting a murderously cold aura.

 

“You guys can drop the act already. Speak, why did you lead me here? Is it to estrange the relationship between Gu Changge and I, so I would go against him? If so, you guys are deluding yourselves.” Gu Xianer’s voice was cold without a hint of kindness.

 

Hearing this, Qin Wuya and Zhi Yang looked at each other, furrowing their brows. Following after, Zhi Yang sighed, “My Elderest Senior Brother and I are doing this out of concern for you.”

 

He decided to lay his cards on the table. Meanwhile, Qin Wuya was silent. They knew it was useless to say anything else at this time. Their previous plan was now completely useless.

 

Gu Xianer furrowed her brows and asked, “What do you mean?”

 

“It’s like everything you just heard, Gu Changge is the Successor of Demonic Arts. He will definitely harm you one day and consume your Source along with everything else. Furthermore, you are our Junior Sister from the previous life. You just don’t remember at the moment.” Sky King Zhi Yang soon continued after taking a breath. “Do you still remember that day, in front of the entrance of the Immortal Academy? I acted that way because I wanted to help you restore your memories from the previous life. If you don’t believe me, I can’t do anything about it.”

 

Sky King Zhi Yang regained his composure, his eyes fixed on Gu Xianer. He planned to tell Gu Xianer everything he knew and heard.

 

“Previous life?” Gu Xianer wrinkled her brows tighter after hearing this. It was impossible to tell her thoughts from her expression.

 

Truthfully speaking, what Sky King Zhi Yang said had shocked her for quite a bit. She had already guessed what Sky King Zhi Yang and Qin Wuya’s intentions were, but for him to say this, it was far outside of her expectation.

 

“In our previous life, we were the disciples of the Borderless Sect. I was the Eldest Senior Brother. You and Junior Brother Zhi Yang were Master’s direct inheritance disciples and your name in your previous life was Dao Xian.” Qin Wuya also opened his mouth this time. He hoped these words could help Gu Xianer remember.

 

However, Gu Xianer’s expression was as cold as always, with any changes. She did not remember anything.

 

“Are you guys done talking?” She asked in an icy tone.

 

The name Dao Xian, was something she had heard Qin Wuya mentioned before. She just didn’t expect it to be her name in her past life. It was just too unbelievable and absurd. [Does reincarnation really exist in the first place?]

 

“This is water from the Lake of Reincarnation, it can restore someone’s memory from their past life. If you don’t believe us, you can drink this.” Qin Wuya spoke up again. He took out a small white jade bottle from his sleeve and handed it to Gu Xianer.

 

It gave off a dazzling, mystical white light that filled the air and the Power of Reincarnation emitted from it, making a shocking sight. In fact, just the bottle alone was enough for people to feel the power surging from it.

 

“Water from the Lake of Reincarnation?” Gu Xianer knitted her brows. She didn’t take the bottle. She could not find it in her to trust Qin Wuya and Sky King Zhi Yang. Similarly, she didn’t even have a way to confirm if it really was water from the Lake of Reincarnation. [Who knows what is actually inside this bottle? But, I can’t say that I’m not curious about what they’ve said. Is it truly what these two want? Setting aside whether the matter about my previous life is a lie, luring me here hardly makes them seem as though they hold any good intentions.]

 

“If you don’t believe us, this is what I saw when I used the Foreseeing Eye.” Seeing Gu Xianer’s silence, Sky King Zhi Yang thought she was thinking about their words seriously and his eyes were filled with joy. He couldn’t help but add on.

 

At the same time, a golden mark appeared between his brows, the brilliance illuminating everything was incomparably resplendent and filled with divinity. He was unable to use the full ability of the Foreseeing Eye right now. However, recreating the scene he saw before wasn’t a difficult thing for him.

 

He believed that if Gu Xianer could see it, she would definitely become suspicious of Gu Changge. At the very least, she wouldn’t trust him unconditionally like she was now.

 

*Buzz!* While speaking, a void formed before Sky King Zhi Yang and suddenly rays of light appeared. Following after, a scenery began forming. In a dim and wet dungeon, a graceful maiden covered in dust was pierced by chains. Her four limbs hung from the wall and there were bloodstains everywhere. Her breath was weak, but her expression was frigid and stubborn.

 

The handsome man in front of her wearing clothes purer than snow looked like a young noble untouched by dust. The small, white jade bowl in his hand was filled with blood that emitted an evil aura.

 

Seeing this, Gu Xianer who had been silent suddenly trembled, her complexion turning white as if she was completely drained of blood.

 

“What a load of bullshit, I’m going to kill you.” Suddenly, she coldly spoke and her jade sword flew out from her palm. It was clear and sparkling, giving off a resplendent light, with a fearsome sharp tip. It sliced through space, heading straight towards Zhi Yang with every intention to kill him.

 

“This is the future I saw using my Foreseeing Eye. Gu Changge harbors ill intentions towards you, he is the real Successor of Demonic Arts, why won’t you believe us? Eldest Senior Brother and I would never hurt you.” Sky King Zhi Yang’s expression was heavy and he couldn’t help asking. At the same time, he waved his sleeve and a purple aura rushed out like the Milky Way, crashing down from the sky to counter Gu Xianer’s attack.

 

With his current strength, if Gu Xianer didn’t have other tricks up her sleeve, it wouldn’t be hard for him to subdue her. However, Sky King Zhi Yang didn’t do so, and continued trying to mend the situation. He hoped the Gu Xianer could think it through and no longer stick to the wrong path.

 

“Don’t you guys know how to do anything else besides sabotaging my relationship with Gu Changge? Suppose that’s my future, so what, I’m willing.” Gu Xianer’s voice was still cold as frost without any kind of emotion.

 

*Bang!* The jade sword cut through space and its stray slashes demolished everything around causing the mountain range to crumble. The sword carried incredible Principles, which were turbulent and boundless. It emitted all sorts of brilliant radiance.

 

Hearing her words, Sky King Zhi Yang’s expression became extremely ugly; there was anger, gloominess, hatred, but above all, there was envy and unwillingness.

 

“Why, just why? I am the one who treats you well and Gu Changge only wants to harm you…” He was practically roaring in anger, he didn’t think Gu Xianer would say something like that. He could feel his whole body being engulfed by the flames of envy. At this moment, Sky King Zhi Yang couldn’t care about anything anymore. He was going to suppress Gu Xianer and make her drink the water from the Lake of Reincarnation.

 

Suddenly, the surrounding Principles interwove, forming divine chains in the sky. The brilliant light filling the entire space in an instant was terrifying to behold.

 

Purple mist shrouded the air, forming a new boundless layer among the clouds. In the midst of it, all sorts of divine abilities were manifesting along with the resonating crackles of the true purple lightning that sounded like it would tear everything apart.

 

Gu Xianer was also using all sorts of methods to counter this situation, all while maintaining her frigid expression.

 

Qin Wuya never made a move, he only watched from the side while containing the fluctuations from the fight so they wouldn’t attract or alert other.

 

*Rumble!* The battle was becoming increasingly shocking. The sound of thunder was like an Immortal beating a drum. It shook the very earth, completely demolishing the surrounding terrain, and rendering the ruins to debris.

 

Countless Principles continued to manifest, and were congealing,

 

Gu Xianer’s Celestial Bone shone and seemed to have transform her into a supreme True Immortal, one who could suppress everything.

 

Sky King Zhi Yang’s Chaos Dao Bone was also changing, his purple aura was vast and boundless like a cascading Milky Way. In the end, a reverberating sound echoed through the air and dark red blood splattered everywhere.

 

Gu Xianer was sent flying, there was blood on the corner of her mouth and her expression was like ice.

 

In the end, her cultivation level was much lower than Sky King Zhi Yang’s, after exchanging many moves, it quickly became disadvantageous for her. She wasn’t his opponent.

 

“Junior Sister, you really can’t beat me. Don’t resist anymore and just listen…” Sky King Zhi Yang’s purple robes fluttered as he stood in the sky. At this moment, after having secured victory, his expression had returned to normal. He looked at her and said this.

 

However, before he finished speaking, behind him, Qin Wuya’s expression abruptly changed and he yelled, “This is bad, dodge!”

 

*Bang!* At the very next moment, a terrifying strength blasted. Mountains collapsed and the whole world shook, as space itself was blown open.

 

Sky King Zhi Yang failed to react in time. Alarmed, he tried to turn around, but he was slammed by that power. There was a sound of an explosion, of his body being destroyed, and his flesh spewing everywhere.

 

“You’re seeking death You dare injure her?” A figure walked out from the void and a palm fell from the sky, seemingly carrying the anger of heaven, possessing incomparable strength.

 

His expression was like a frozen wasteland; it made goosebumps rise on everyone’s backs. It made them feel cold from head to toe as their souls tremble in fear.
