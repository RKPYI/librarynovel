In response, Gu Changge chuckled aloud. “Why? Are you going to turn into another entity once you drink it? Or, will it grant you the power to beat me?”

 

“I am not talking about that. Don’t change the subject.” Gu Xianer furiously glared at him, knowing he was digressing from the subject on purpose.

 

“You know, some decisions are yours to make yourself, since you’re the one who chose this. Besides, I believe you’re curious as to whether Qin Wuya and Sky King Zhi Yang were telling the truth.” Gu Changge withdrew his smile and revealed a rare stern expression, as if he was contemplating for her sake.

 

Hearing that, Gu Xianer looked at him deeply. It was as Gu Changge had stated, she was eager to find out the validity of Sky King Zhi Yang’s words, and she wondered if reincarnation actually existed. Previously, she didn’t accept the water from the Lake of Reincarnation out of concern that the water might be dangerous, but now that Gu Changge assured her, it must mean that the water was fine.

 

After a moment of consideration, Gu Xianer pursed her lips and made up her mind. She finally accepted the little white bottle. However, the next moment, her actions were even more direct as she, without any hesitation, turned the bottle upside down and poured the water onto the ground.

 

*Splash!* Along with the splash, a silver flow of vibrant light gushed out from the flask into the air. It was as if the Milky Way was plummeting. The water from the Lake of Reincarnation glowed dazzlingly, carrying wondrous runes, but the moment it was exposed to the air, it vanished without a trace, as if it was vaporised.

 

Seemingly surprised by that, Gu Changge painfully asked, “Did you have to pour it all out?”

 

Coldly, Gu Xianer rolled her eyes at him. “So what? Did you really want me to drink it?”

 

“What an idiot.” Gu Changge heaved a sigh. “If you weren’t going to drink it anyway, why didn’t you just return it to me? Water from the Lake of Reincarnation, wasted just like that. Jeez, Qin Wuya must have paid a great price to retrieve this small bottle.”

 

“Aren’t you missing the point here? Are you seriously upset over some lake water?” Gu Xianer stared at him, disaffected. She thought this decision of hers would cause Gu Changge to have a new perception of her, and perhaps even be happy or comforted about it. That he would give her a compliment or two, but evidently, that wasn’t the case.

 

[Is he genuinely heartbroken over this small bottle of water? Do I mean less than the water to him!?]

 

Looking at her praise-seeking expression, Gu Changge subconsciously laughed. “You threw away my Lake of Reincarnation water, and you’re expecting compliments? Gu Xianer, are you confusing your dreams with reality? You’d certainly wish you were as beautiful as your dreams.”

 

“Gu Changge, you accursed bastard! All you do is say mean things to me! If you think I’ll ever entertain you again, you’re wrong!” Gu Xianer grew infuriated, her body trembling out of rage. Though, her petite body didn’t quite display her trembling. Nonetheless, after she rebuked him, she blinked out of the area and disappeared.

 

[‘You’d certainly wish you were as beautiful as your dreams,’ he said? Was he saying that I’m ugly?] Gu Xianer wanted to continue rebuking him after voicing her fierceful remarks, but after witnessing Gu Changge’s strength, she decided not to do so. After some pondering, the only threatening words she could come up with were those that she said.

 

Shaking his head, Gu Changge watched as she left before withdrawing his smile. Truthfully, even if Gu Xianer were to drink water from the Lake of Reincarnation, nothing would happen. Otherwise, he wouldn’t have been handed the flask over to her so assuredly.

 

Given his personality, he would never do anything that was out of his control. Even if Gu Xianer were to restore her memories from her past life, and thereby get attached to her Seniors from her past life, she would only have two choices. Either to oppose him or sever her relationship with them.

 

Either way, both choices were too cruel to Gu Xianer, and Gu Changge didn’t want her to have to stain her hands with the blood of her loved ones, as that would be outside of the kind and cheeky image he had of her in his mind. Therefore, discarding the water of the Lake of Reincarnation was indeed the best choice for her. But of course, the result would be the same even if she were to drink it.

Hence, it bore no traces or imprintment from her previous life. In other words, Gu Changge destroyed the only way Gu Xianer could regain her memories from her past life a long time ago.

 

After the exchange with Gu Xianer, Gu Changge stopped minding her. It had to be said he simply looked for a residence and kept her there, after he kidnapped her.

 

Given Qing Xiaoyi’s cultivation level, she would remain intact even if she were to be confined for years. Though, since he did not directly extract it from her, she wasn’t hurt during the process.

After all, Gu Lintian would never bother them with a trivial matter such as Purple Mansion.
