Chapter 586, She Can Learn, the Troubles Encountered by the Mortal Palace

From Gu Changge’s point of view, the Ji Siblings’ problem was actually very simple. It could be resolved with just a single sentence from him, stating that the Ji Family was under his wing.

 

That would be enough to make their enemies tremble in fear and dare not attack the Ji Family. Thus, when he allowed Ji Chuyue to stay, it was tantamount to agreeing to their request of sending a signal to the outside world. Afterall, there were many Forces eyeing the Ji Family. Now that Ji Chuyue and Ji Yaoxing had suddenly left the Ji Family and came to the Gu Family, many hid and watched their every move.

 

In the end, Ji Chuyue stayed here, while Ji Yaoxing returned alone. What would this mean? As the princess of the Ji Family who was out and about, Ji Chuyue was a representative of the Ji Family. She had reached marriageable age without being engaged and had moved in with the Gu family. Even a fool would know what this meant.

 

Ji Yaoxing and Ji Chuyue weren’t fools and instantly understood Gu Changge’s intentions.

 

“Thank you, Young Master Gu”

 

“The Ji Family will forever remember your kindness.”

 

The expressions of the Ji Siblings were filled with utmost appreciation as their voices quavered, clueless as to how to fully express their gratitude.

 

Before they came, they were already mentally prepared for Gu Changge’s rejection. Who would’ve thought that he would agree? This act of kindness alone was enough for all the members of the Ji Family to remember forever. Not to mention, Gu Changge was the one who helped the siblings escape Kun Mountain.

 

[We owe Young Master Gu too much.] Ji Yaoxing thought to himself as he glanced at his sister who was blushing. Ji Chuyue understood what her brother meant and nodded shyly. Her cheeks felt hot and her heart beat faster. She didn’t even dare to raise her head to look at Gu Changge.

 

“Senior Ji Shengchu once showed great kindness to all living beings. Now that the Ji Family is in trouble, how can I stand by and just watch?” Gu Changge didn’t turn back, but sighed, as if he felt sorry for what happened to the Ji Family.

 

Ji Yaoxing continued, “No matter what, the Ji Family will forever remember the fact that Young Master Gu is willing to lend a helping hand to the Gu Family at such a point in time.”

 

Before, he had always been arrogant and thought that there was no one stronger than him in this generation. Although he carried a humble appearance, he had always thought that if the Ji Family regained its past power and influence, he would definitely become one of the most powerful young prodigies in the world.

 

Previously, he was afraid of Gu Changge due to his power and status, but now, he was filled with pure admiration towards him.

 

Gu Changge smiled and waved his sleeve, “Don’t be like this, brother Yaoxing. This is just a piece of cake.”

 

Although Gu Changge told them not to take things so seriously, both were extremely grateful towards him.

 

After he had obtained Gu Changge’s reassurance, the knot in Ji Yaoxing’s heart finally came undone. He let out a long sigh of relief as he left. Finally, his face, which had been filled with sorrow the whole day, had a smile filled with confidence.

 

“Brother…” Seeing her brother leave, Ji Chuyue suddenly felt uneasy. She was at a loss and didn’t even know where to put her hands. Afterall, Gu Changge and her were the only ones left in this palace.

 

Gu Changge’s Divine Island was where he usually cultivated. From young, Ji Chuyue was spoiled. Although she didn’t have the temper of a spoiled young lady, she had never served anyone. Now that she stood behind Gu Changge, it felt as though a beam of lights shone on her back. She was restless and was at a loss as to what to do. Since he had asked her to stay…

 

Now, her head was filled with what Gu Changge said just now.

 

Following her muddled thoughts, her little pair of hands twisted together.

 

The first time she saw Gu Changge was at Purple Mountains, when she was searching for the Tower of World Denomination. Back then, Gu Changge and his fiancee, the Fourth Princess of the Unrivalled Celestial Dynasty, Yue Mingkong.

 

Purple Mountains was also where she first met Jiang Chen. When she found out that he couldn’t join the Heavenly Ruin Gate due to his age and poor cultivation level. Thus, she gave him a chance.

 

It was also then when Jiang Chen confessed to her how he was a Successor of a Divine Source Master and could help her find the Tower of World Denomination.

 

As Ji Chuyue’s priority back then was to restore the Ji Family’s former glory, she naturally chose to believe Jiang Chen. At that time, she thought that as the Successor of the Gu Family, Gu Changge was too powerful to be from the same world as her. Even though she was respectful towards him, she was still indifferent towards him. Even after she found out that he was looking for the Tower of World Domination, she went behind his back and went to Purple Mountains with Jiang Chen.

 

However, that was the incident that made her realise what true power meant.

 

After their trail and scheme was found out by Chen Ning’er, the Ji Family was coerced into giving Gu Changge the Wheel of World Domination, which they had painstakingly gotten. They sent it as an offering to appease his anger, afraid of offending him.

 

Originally, there wasn’t much of an exchange between Ji Chuyue and Gu Changge. Never in her wildest dreams would she have thought that Gu Changge would become the saviour of the Ji Family or that she would be so shy and nervous as she stood behind him today. All of this could just be written off as a joke of fate.

 

She was clear that after what happened at Kun Mountain, she had already developed an indescribable admiration towards this man. He was a perfect young man without any flaws.

 

Ji Chuyue believed that in this world, as long as women have had contact with him, it would be impossible for them not to develop such thoughts. Despite him teasing her in front of Jiang Chen at Dark Kun City, she was never truly angry. All she felt was a little uncomfortable and embarrassed.

 

“Don’t worry. After today, all Forces won’t dare to do anything to the Ji Family.” Gu Changge laughed, as if he had felt Ji Chuyue’s nervousness.

 

As she listened to his soft voice that felt unnaturally warm, Ji Chuyue unconsciously became more unrelaxed. “Okay,”

 

Ji Chuyue nodded like a chicken pecking at rice. She replied in a mosquito-like voice, “Chuyue doesn’t know how to repay Young Master Gu’s kindness…”

 

Gu Changge laughed and turned around to interrupt her, “So you plan to repay me with your body?”

 

Ji Chuyue never expected Gu Changge to be so direct. Her cheeks grew redder and she looked down, shyer than before.

 

She was suddenly reminded of something an old grandma said back then. About what to do when she meets a man who has shown great kindness towards her based on his appearance. If the man looks average, then she should be his slave in her next life. If he looks handsome, then she should repay him with her body. Back then, she innocently thought that the old grandma was just joking, but today, she didn’t know why such thoughts would come to her mind as she looked at Gu Changge.

 

“Young Master Gu, I don’t think you lack a woman like Chuyue by your side, but if you don’t mind…” She lifted her head and carefully observed Gu Changge’s expression, as if she was afraid of saying something wrong that would cause him to be unhappy.

 

Instead, Gu Changge laughed without a care. [If Jiang Chen was alive to see this scene, I wonder how he would feel?]

 

Actually, Ji Chuyue’s Fortuity wasn’t low at all. In fact, she might’ve been one of Jiang Chen’s heroines.

 

However, now that Jiang Chen was dead, there was no need for Gu Changge to care about what would happen to her in the future. If this was in the past, Gu Changge would’ve thought about accumulating some Fortuity or Fatum, but now he temporarily doesn’t need all of this.

 

“Come here and chat.” Gu Changge smiled gently as he urged Ji Chuyue to come closer.

 

“Yes…Young Master Gu.” Ji Chuyue stammered slightly as she walked towards him with her head buried, revealing her white and slender neck, like a frightened little deer in a forest.

 

“Do you still remember what I said to you at Dark Kun City?” Gu Changge smiled as he reached out to caress her hair.

 

“I still… still remember.” Ji Chuyue nodded her head. Of course she wouldn’t forget the scene that day.

 

“Then, do you know why your brother insisted on you coming?” As if interested, Gu Changge asked.

 

Naturally, Ji Chuyue didn’t dare to lie. She could only reply honestly in a quivering voice, “Because…brother wanted me to curry favour with Young Master Gu, so that you will save our Ji Family.”

 

“Oh? Looks like you’re quite honest.” Gu Changge laughed.

 

Ji Chuyue thought that he wanted to blame her brother, thus she quickly looked up at him and explained, “ Young Master Gu, we’re really grateful that you didn’t give us a hard time and even agreed to help us…:

 

“But I don’t think you came here willingly. Don’t worry, I won’t force you to stay here, you may return to the Ji Family after some time.” As he listened to her, Gu Changge replied with the same gentle smile.

 

Ji Chuyue didn’t think that Gu Changge would say that. She was stunned and her heart became sour and complicated for a while.

 

In her eyes, this was another way for Gu Changge to reject her. After all, how could she be forced or unwilling to do something like this? Even though their family was in peril, her brother would never mistreat her and force her to be with someone who would do harm to her. Even if it was for the sake of the Family, there was a line that even they would not cross.

 

“Chuyue knows that she’s clumsy, dumb and doesn’t know how to please Young Master Gu, but she can learn all of these. The things that Qingxuan and the rest can do, Chuyue can also do too. I’m definitely not inferior to them.” As if she had deliberated for a long time, Ji Chuyue suddenly mustered up the courage and looked up at Gu Changge, wanting him to give her a chance.

 

However, all Gu Changge did was laugh at her. [If you want to learn, you can take the time to learn, but I dont’ have the time to entertain you.]

 

After Ji Yaoxing returned to the Ji Family, the matters of the Ji Family were naturally resolved.

 

Currently, in the Upper Realm, no Force dared to provoke Gu Changge. Since he wants to protect the Ji Family, no one dared to even think about touching the Ji Family.

 

In the following days, Ji Chuyue seemed to have also adapted to her new lifestyle. She had put aside her status as the Ji Family’s princess and had started to ask the maids who usually served Gu Changge about his various habits and preferences. She believed that if given time, even water droplets would create a hole in the hardest rock. She believed that one day, she would definitely move Gu Changge.

 

However, not long after, Gu Changge left the Gu Family with Ah Da and headed for the territory where the Mortal Palace was located.

 

He wasn’t in a hurry since he hadn’t received any news about the owner of the Sword of World Domination from Yue Mingkong. Coincidentally, at that time, the Eternal Overcast crisis was getting even more dire. Thus, Gu Changge used his identity as the Mortal One as justification for his trip there.

 

Meanwhile, Jiang Chuchu had found a few areas where the Eternal Overcast had spread, causing chaos. It was just that, with her current abilities, she couldn’t do anything about it.

 

However, if Gu Changge wanted to obtain Eternal Overcast Sources, he would have to go to her.

 

…

 

At the same time, in the heart of the Sky Domain within the Upper Realm, inside one of the most prosperous ancient territories, a palace stood tall in the skies. It was majestic and sacred, like a palace built from the legends.

 

Built with magnificent and intricate craftsmanship, its walls were covered in flawlessly glazed white jade tiles. Its eaves were made out of millions-year-old purple gold sandalwood and its floors were pure obsidian star marble.

 

It was grand yet simple, majestic yet solemn.

 

Within it, there was a thick sea of celestial energy, just like in the Celestial Realm. The palace overlooked the ancient city and endless rivers. As runes intertwined above, the main hall rose and fell with the sun and the moon. Every moment, it reflected the light of the stars, the heavens and earth. It was like the home of a supreme god.

 

A huge celestial plaque was hung in front of the main hall. With purple as its base and Divine Gold as characters, two ancient words were written, ‘Mortal Palace’.

 

Tens of thousands of silver threads from between the heavens and earth, frantically rushed and converged into this place, making it appear sacred and white. These silver threads were the worship and congratulations of all the living beings between heaven and earth. It was for the Mortal One’s great achievements.

 

At that time, a stunning woman, who wore immaculate white robes and a veil, took a seat in the main hall. Her skin was fair and delicate, her eyebrows were pieces of art. Her eyes were pristine black gems that shone with a lustre. Still, her expression was calm. Besides calmness, there were no other emotions. It was as though she was born without emotions and desires. This was one of the two Saintess of the Mortal Palace, Jiang Chuchu.

 

In front of her, there was a Golden Fortuity Crown, where all the silver threads rushed into. She was using this Golden Fortuity Cauldron to cultivate.

 

Like a rumbling ocean, strange sounds of karma resonated throughout the palace. This was the artifact that Gu Changge had left in the Mortal Palace. Turns out, Jiang Chuchu had found it and used it to cultivate, allowing her to make tremendous progress.

 

*Boom!*

 

As she cultivated, many strange sights materialised in the air. One by one, blurry shadows of Immortals, which were grand yet mysterious, floated behind her. It was as if they had travelled across time and space.

 

At this moment, an old woman in white robes rushed in hurriedly, with a worried expression, “Saintess, there is news of an Eternal Overcast outbreak in the southwest direction of the Sky Domain! The Great Yu Celestial Dynasty has already sent their men to investigate! Should we also send someone to take a look?”

 

As she listened, Jiang Chuchu furrowed her brows. She stopped cultivating and asked, “How many crises have occurred due to the Eternal Overcast in just this month alone?”

 

Although she sped up on her cultivation efforts every day and night, she was still powerless in the face of the sudden news of these crises.
