The following morning, Gu Changge, Jiang Chuchu, Chosen Monk Jin Chan and several others stood on a mountain outside the Floating Buddhist Temple, watching the deep abyss swirling with Demonic Qi in the distance. The dark purple Demonic Qi surged violently as it spread, resembling dark clouds blocking the sky. It was like a darker world where no light could be seen.

 

The Demon-Burying Abyss was more like a doorway than an abyss. The terrifying entrance was like a giant wound slicing across the air, emanating frightening energy. Entering it would be like entering a dark and silent world. Piles of bodies could be seen inside the entrance, some as big as mountains. Even dead, some of the bodies continued to glow like tiny suns while emitting heat. However, those bodies were covered in hole-like injuries due to pollution by Demonic Qi.

 

There were many powerful arrays near the Abyss, expelling strong beams of divine light which transformed into swords, blades, and tridents as they shot into the abyss to prevent more from escaping. Many strong cultivators could be seen around the Demon-Burying Abyss; while they didn’t dare stand near it, they fought with the great demons inside.

 

They weren’t here because they wanted to protect the peace of the land but because killing the great demons could allow them to gain material containing crystals with high concentrations of energy. Those included the demonic core, skull, finger bones, and other materials which often fetched high prices in the market.

 

Demonic cores could be used to meld artifacts and set up arrays but could only be commonly found near the Demon-Burying Abyss, making them extremely rare. That was why many talented and brave cultivators risked their lives to come here.

 

These cultivators had noticed Gu Changge’s group and were shocked by them, not expecting others to come other than them. However, some quickly recognised Gu Changge and became surprised yet puzzled. [Why had he come when he has such a noble status?]

 

“Why is Young Master Changge here? Is he planning on dealing with the source of this chaos? I guess that does fit his character.”

 

Their expressions became respectful and admiring. How many of the younger generation would risk their lives to come here? They admired such bravery and selfless behaviour. They wouldn’t have set foot here if not for money constraints, but here he was walking into the lion’s den.

 

“That’s the entrance of the Demon-Burying Abyss. A powerful monk from the Buddhist Mountain set up an array there to prevent demonic entities from escaping. Follow me closely and don’t fall into the array,” Jin Chan told them. He was much calmer after talking with the elderly monk last night.

 

He led the group from the Buddhist Mountain as they transformed into divine rainbows and moved forward. Beams of golden light shot from the peak of the mountain. The great cloud of Demonic Qi surging towards him was repelled by the Buddha aura from the Buddha beads in his hand.
