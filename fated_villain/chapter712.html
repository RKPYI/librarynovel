I Am The Fated Villain

Chapter 712, Seriously Pique My Interest, Go the Extra Mile

Translator: Fate

Proofreader: Silavin

 

*Crack!* Delicate yet evident cracks prominently appeared on the Reincarnation Platform. The cracks then grew bigger, quickly spreading throughout the entire platform, after which golden lights erupted followed by a blaring explosion that blew the platform apart.

 

“What?” The people of the Eternal Overcast Kingdom were instantly stunned as their gleeful faces immediately disappeared.

 

“Shattered?” Jia Nan widened her eyes in dismay as she peered at the cracks, doubting her own eyes.

 

[Impossible! Why would the Reincarnation Platform shatter and turn to dust out of nowhere?] In that instant, her heart bore nothing but disbelief.

 

Even the Six Great Ancestors, who were observing everything from the homeland, lost their cool. They were deeply affected by this. If it weren’t for the restrictions, they would have shown themselves and personally looked into the destruction of the Reincarnation Platform. [Was this merely an accident? Or did Gu Changge rig it on purpose?]

 

“If he indeed pulled off some tricks…” One of the Ancestors was visibly sullen, his face extremely taut, while the other five Ancestors were similarly morose, glared at Gu Changge with hostility.

 

“What was the problem? Why did it suddenly break? Ah, it seems the Life Rocks and Death Rocks were all for nothing. I’ll have to do this once more.” Feigning ignorance, Gu Changge shook his head and vented his sympathy.

 

Looking at his expression, the crowd had no idea that he spoke those words intentionally. [Did he not cause the destruction of the Reincarnation Platform?]

 

“I’m afraid we’ll have to trouble you once more, Young Master Gu.” Despite her desire to question Gu Changge about the shattering of the Reincarnation Platform, Jia Nan took a deep breath to collect herself and spoke in a calm tone.

 

Though, she still suspected that Gu Changge might have deliberately broken the platform. [But why would he do that for no good reason?]

 

“All good, though the Life Rocks and Death Rocks have been wasted.” Gu Changge gently shook his head, seemingly sympathetic.

 

Jia Nan looked at him deeply and wordlessly nodded. Since the Six Great Ancestors remained silent, she dared not to make any decision.

 

The remaining Eternal Overcast Beings didn’t dare to say anything, yet judging from their eyes, they were obviously concerned.

 

For the next few days, this place was shrouded in stunning black and white colours.

 

Gu Changge’s eyes were serenity as though he entered a state of eternal tranquillity. Once again, he condensed the Life Rocks and Death Rocks according to the instructed structure and reconstructed a new Reincarnation Platform.

 

Naturally, he had indeed rigged the previous Reincarnation Platform, causing it to fall apart, in order to test out the attitudes of the Six Great Ancestors. [They are surprisingly patient.]

 

As such, Gu Changge could tell that the Reincarnation Platform was of absolute importance to the Six Great Ancestors.

 

[This seems to be a mere coordinate that is unable to communicate the mysteries of Reincarnation. Do they perhaps intend to draw someone here?] Gu Changge was genuinely taken aback. The secrets of Eternal Overcast must be deeper than what he could tell right now.

 

[In that case, I shall drop the courtesy!] Having thought things through, he glanced at the depth of the Eternal Overcast homeland and revealed a subtle grin.

 

*Whoosh!*

 

Right away, the Power of Reincarnation intertwined around the hall. Spring, summer, autumn, and winter—the four seasons densely passed along with the evolution of all lives. Compared to the first attempt to forge the Reincarnation Platform, the aura this time was significantly more intense.

 

The surrounding people felt as if they were being absorbed into Reincarnation. Eventually, the entire Eternal Overcast was affected by the aura.

 

Blinding lights beamed towards the clouds, thoroughly illuminating the dark sky. Even the depths of the homeland, which was covered in grey mists, was affected by the aura.

 

At that moment, everyone could clearly see six ambiguous yet frightening figures standing afar. The six figures possessed daunting, unrelenting auras that could subdue all Principles of the world as if they had existed since the beginning of time.

 

“The Six Great Ancestors! They have reawakened as expected!” Seeing that, countless Eternal Overcast Beings immediately knelt down towards their direction.

 

The Six Great Ancestors never expected that Gu Changge would reveal them with the Reincarnation Platform. Though, in that instant, as they no longer had anything to hide, they silently stood still, gazing upon all living beings with cold, suffocating, intimidating eyes.

 

As the founding Ancestors of the Eternal Overcast, their existence led to the birth of Eternal Overcast Being, followed by the creation of all other things. Without a doubt, they were the unrivalled supreme divine beings to all Eternal Overcast Beings.

 

*Whoosh!*

 

At that moment, under the reflection of the aura of the Reincarnation Platform, the surrounding void and Principles became blurry. It was as if they couldn’t contain the aura of the Six Great Ancestors’ appearance, and seemed to be breaking apart.

 

“Young man, did you expose us on purpose?” One of the Ancestors walked out from the Eternal Overcast homeland. With his entire body shrouded in grey mists, he gazed upon Gu Changge. His voice was cold and emotionless as if it was an enormous bell that reverberated the entire universe.

 

From the beginning, they never had the intention to reveal themselves. Even after that night when they caught Gu Changge sneaking into the depths of the homeland, they did not pursue him.

 

Now, however, with how Gu Changge exposed them right before all Eternal Overcast Beings, they felt deeply humiliated. Thus, in that instant, if they remained quiet for any longer, Gu Changge would surely belittle them, thoroughly embarrassing themselves in front of their worshippers.

 

*Boom!*

 

All of a sudden, a terrifying, suppressing aura erupted, as if the sky was collapsing. The surrounding World Principles and void fell apart as energy and illumination were waning.

 

“This is bad… The Great Ancestors are enraged…” Terrified, the Eternal Overcast Beings shivered in restlessness and got on their knees, while an overwhelming aura vigorously subdued them.

 

“The Great Ancestors are truly enraged! The situation is getting out of control!” The faces of Jia Nan and the rest grew taut. They felt suffocated as if someone was forcibly grabbing their hearts.

 

Since ancient times, the depths of the Eternal Overcast had been desolate. Rarely had their Great Ancestor showed themselves, let alone one got angered. Thus, to all Eternal Overcast Beings, this was a tall tale—the first that to be recorded in ancient archives.

 

“Losing your temper already?” Nonetheless, facing the wrath of an Ancestor of the Eternal Overcast, Gu Changge retained his casual, composed look. He slowly lifted his palm off the Reincarnation Platform, as if nothing shocking was happening.

 

At this point, he indeed deliberately exposed the Six Great Ancestors right in front of the Eternal Overcast Beings. Since they loved spying on everything in the dark, he decided not to let them have their way. Moreover, he wanted to make it clear to them that he was now the one in control.

 

When Gu Changge first came to Eternal Overcast, he was not aware of the heritage of the place nor the intentions of the Six Great Ancestors, so he refrained from startling them. Nonetheless, now that he understood the importance of the Reincarnation Platform to the Eternal Overcast and the true strength of the Six Great Ancestors, he no longer had anything to worry about.

 

“Nevermind. As long as you forge the Reincarnation Platform, we swear on our Dao Heart that we shall not trouble you.” Then, another one of the Six Great Ancestors seemed to have seen through Gu Changge’s intention and spoke with a calm voice.

 

Hearing that, the Ancestor who stepped out from the depths of the homeland earlier halted his steps. After all, before the Reincarnation Platform was successfully forged, it was in their best interest not to have any conflicts with Gu Changge. Their gestures were only to intimidate Gu Changge in order to prevent him from playing any more tricks.

 

If they really wanted to take action, there would have been more than one Ancestor stepping out of the depths of the homeland.

 

Meanwhile, the other Great Ancestors remained as icy as a Devil Mountain, wordlessly standing at the brink of the homeland, all while emitting indescribable intimidation that left all suffocating.

 

“Trouble me? Are you even capable of doing that, though?” Hearing that, Gu Changge faintly grinned, his expression completely unbothered and casual.

 

Upon those words, not only were Jia Nan and the rest stunned, but even the nearby Eternal Overcast Beings were totally stupefied, feeling extremely anxious and dismayed.

 

Close to being gods, the Six Great Ancestors were unrivalled beings that created Eternal Overcast. No one dared to defy them. In the eyes of the Eternal Overcast Beings, Gu Changge’s actions were insane and inconceivable.

 

“Young man, are you provoking us?” With that, the Great Ancestor that stepped out from the Eternal Overcast homeland revealed a chilly expression. He never expected Gu Changge to mock and provoke them right in front of all Eternal Overcast Beings, as if he had not the slightest respect for them.

 

*Boom!*

 

The next moment, frightening grey mists violently surged from the depths of the homeland as if they were a raging tsunami. Engulfing the sky with its uncontrollable, relentless current, it was about to consume everything in existence.

 

Evidently, Gu Changge’s words angered them. In their whole undying lives, this is the first time being provoked by a person, and a young one at that.

 

*Boom!* Terrifying auras gushed into existence while the land and void were collapsing, as if the depths of Eternal Overcast fell into eternal silence.

 

As such, all Eternal Overcast Beings trembled intensely, feeling the urge to kneel down as fear seeped into their souls.

 

“I shall teach you of reverence today!” With a glacial glare, the Great Ancestor drew his giant palm covered in grey hair towards Gu Changge.

 

At the same time, the other Great Ancestor who spoke earlier, too, drew his hands. One after another, Divine Chains that were as massive as mountains appeared behind his body, penetrating the sky and shattering all Daos, as if they were sharp arrows that were to be shot through Gu Changge.

 

Instead of going for the fatal blow, the Great Ancestors merely wanted to intimidate Gu Changge and make him realise that they were the true dominators of this world. After all, they still needed Gu Changge to forge the Reincarnation Platform, so they couldn’t afford to kill him now.

 

In the depths of the silent, desolate Eternal Overcast homeland, all of the Six Great Ancestors revealed themselves. Two of them then declared their intention to teach Gu Changge a lesson.

 

Such a sight left all Eternal Overcast Beings shaking violently. After all, the Six Great Ancestors were unequalled beings who created and nurtured everyone of the Eternal Overcast. They were undying beings who coexisted with time itself.

 

“Took the words right out of my mouth. Given that the Eternal Overcast is at the brink of total withering, I believe you old things won’t live for much longer.” Unaffected by the intimidation, Gu Changge retained his subtle smile and straightforwardly exposed the truth which the Six Great Ancestors had been hiding.

 

Of course, those words were not spoken baselessly. The last few days, he had figured out the source of the Yin Energy within Eternal Overcast, which turned out to be the mysterious homeland.

 

The Six Great Ancestors must have exited seclusion because of a matter that threatened their life, which was why they were so attentive to it.

 

As Gu Changge spoke, the figure of an ancient world occurred behind him, harbouring unlimited power that then burst and deflected all the attacks coming his way.

 

“What!?” Upon Gu Changge’s statement, the Eternal Overcast Beings were dumbfounded, widening their eyes in incredulity. [The Six Great Ancestors won’t live for long?]

 

“Young man, mind your words. We are invincible beings that coexist with the River of Time itself, and we are as durable as the world. How can we possibly die?” The faces of the other Great Ancestors that remained still within the homeland grew cold.

 

They didn’t know how Gu Changge reached such a conclusion, but they would never allow the secrets of the depth of the homeland to be exposed.

 

As long as the water of the ancient wells continued to flow, they would never die. Even if they received fatal injuries, their Life Force waned, or their bodies were disintegrated, they needed only to consume the water from the wells and they would be restored to their intact state. Such was the secret to their undying state.

 

“It seems like I am right. The Source Relic of Eternal Overcast is indeed hidden in that homeland, and it also created you,” Gu Changge said with a knowing grin.

 

At this point, he no longer had to hide his true intentions. Ever since he arrived at the Eternal Overcast, he never had the intention to help its people forge the Reincarnation Platform. Since the origins of the Eternal Overcast has been found, there was no longer a need for him to go the extra mile.

 

“What are you implying?” The Ancestor that stepped out of the homeland furrowed his brows, feeling a surge of distress in his heart, as if he and his peers had underestimated Gu Changge’s audacity and intention.

 

“You old relics seriously pique my interest. Now, even I am curious what else is hidden underneath that homeland of yours,” Gu Changge blandly noted, withdrawing the smile on his face.

 

At once, he stepped forward. His terrorising aura swept down, engulfing the entire Eternal Overcast, which left the surrounding stars shattering. Unable to bear such an aura, even the formidable cultivators of the Eternal Overcast Kingdom coughed up blood before their bodies blew up.

 

“Does he plan on attacking the Great Ancestors?” Seeing that, Jia Nan was frantic with shock and disbelief.

 

Although she was still young, she was highly knowledgeable about the Six Great Ancestors and she understood that they were truly undying beings that coexisted with time itself.

 

Although Gu Changge’s strength was immeasurable, he might ultimately die despite his capability to rival the Six Great Ancestors. Such was the unchangeable truth.

 

“Young man, what are you doing?” The faces of the Six Great Ancestors drastically changed. They never expected that Gu Changge would actually attack them.

 

*Boom!*

 

As Gu Changge stepped out, he simultaneously drew his hand, intending to disperse the grey mists in front of him. As a result, the depths of the Eternal Overcast homeland was instantaneously destroyed.

 

In that instant, without concealing his own aura, Gu Changge swept his palm forward and everything shook before falling apart.

 

Even the surrounding Dao and Principles were broken, as the land fractured. The grey mists instantly vanished, leaving all Eternal Overcast Beings to shiver in absolute dread.
